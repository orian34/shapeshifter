This mod is focused on adding more items to become a shapeshifter. 
Armor sets built from boss remains to impersonate them and gain their abilities and others things.

Content :
-The King Slime Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the ruler of the slimes!
-The Eye of Cthulhu Shapeshifter's Armor. Become rage incarnate!
-The Eater of World Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the corrupted worm!
-The Brain of Cthulhu Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the malicious brain!
-The Queen Bee Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the swarm queen!
-The Skeletron Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the cursed guardian!
-The Wall of Flesh Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the hungry abomination!
-The Mechamalgam Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the energy-powered amalgam!
-The Plantera Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the evergrowing plant!
-The Golem Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the ancient construct!
-The Duke Fishron Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the mutant fish!
-The Moon Lord Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the eldritch god!
-The Lunatic Cultist Shapeshifter's Armor. Become the mystic sorcerer!
